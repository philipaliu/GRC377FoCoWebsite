PAL OUTDOORS manufactures and designs outdoors wear and athletic apparel that is durable for everyday wear. Our apparel is made to last in all conditions. PAL Outdoors represents generational greatness. The PAL initials represents the initials of the founder Philip Aaron Liu as well as his father’s and grandfather’s initials. The climber is hanging onto the “P” rock, the same way the brand holds onto its familial roots.
